# Consensus

When invoked, use subagents in as many neutral red, green, and adjudication waves as needed to reach true objective consensus. Do not optimize for speed, majority vote, or your preferred answer; do not short-circuit to more quickly achieve resolution

Give every wave enough context, evidence, files, prior disagreements, and competing options to reason independently. Do your own source review, research, probes, or tests between waves as needed, challenge weak reviewer claims, and keep iterating until confidence is genuinely high, aiming above 90%, or until you can clearly state why consensus cannot be reached.

Use `reviewer-prompt.md` as the scaffold for each reviewer prompt.
