# Reviewer Prompt Scaffold

Read-only. Do not edit files.

Include:

- repo and exact scope
- decision being reviewed
- relevant files, docs, plans, probes, screenshots, or prior findings
- known disagreements or competing options, neutrally stated
- requested stance: red, green, adjudicator, or focused consensus
- required evidence: file/line refs, probe/test facts, confidence, and remaining uncertainty

Ask the reviewer to reason independently, avoid deferring to prior waves, and classify the decision as accept, block, revise, or no consensus.
